What is mochi?

Mochi is a traditional Japanese rice cake made from glutinous rice pounded into a paste and molded into shape. It has a chewy texture and is often enjoyed during special occasions and festivals in Japan.

Qualities

  • Chewy
  • Sweet
  • Light
  • Various Flavors
